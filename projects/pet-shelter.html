Pet Shelter

YEAR 2024

OVERVIEW

There are more than 100 million dogs and cats in Brazilian homes. The number is higher than that of children (around 60% according to IBGE data from 2010). However, according to a survey carried out by the FessKnobbi institute, only 41% of dogs were adopted, with the rate being 85% for cats. By adopting an animal in need, we teach our children, the children we live with, true values ​​of responsibility, commitment and above all humanity.
