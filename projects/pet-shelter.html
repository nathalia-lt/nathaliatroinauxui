Pet Shelter

YEAR 2024

OVERVIEW

There are more than 100 million dogs and cats in Brazilian homes. The number is higher than that of children (around 60% according to IBGE data from 2010). However, according to a survey carried out by the FessKnobbi institute, only 41% of dogs were adopted, with the rate being 85% for cats. By adopting an animal in need, we teach our children, the children we live with, true values ​​of responsibility, commitment and above all humanity.

ROLE

UI Designer

Front-end Developer

PROCESS

wireframe, high-fi wireframe, prototype

TOOLS

TECHNOLOGIES

PROBLEM

How might we encourage and simplify the process of adopting any type of pet?

GOAL

  • Implement a design that makes it easy and appealing for people to adopt pets.
  • Explore ways to reduce the complexity and time required for adoptions.
  • Deliver a simple and clear adoption process workflow.
  • Create a user-friendly search and filtering for pets.

Takeaways

Impact

More pets find forever homes, reducing the numbers of animal shelter. The platform helps to promote a culture of compassion and responsable pet ownership.

What I learned

By designing a user-centered pet adoption experience, I learned the importance of empathy and understanding in design. I developed my skills in user research, accessibility, and visual design, while also gaining experience in designing for social impact and behavior change. Through this project, I applied design thinking methodologies, such as ideation and prototyping, to create a seamless and engaging experience.


Next steps

  • Refine the design, making it even more effective.
  • Identify new features or functionalities that could enhance the user experience and pet adoption outcomes.
