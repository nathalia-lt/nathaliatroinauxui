YEAR 2024

OVERVIEW

GymPro is a modern gym, developed to serve high-end clients, including services such as gym, spa, swimming pool, minibar and restaurants, personal trainer, extreme sports such as climbing, fights, among other exclusive services.

ROLE

UX/UI Designer

PROCESS

User research, interviews, survey, competitive analysis, ideation, prototyping, testing, evaluation

TOOLS

Figma, Miro

PROBLEM

Despite the fact that it is a gym with high end technology, it became obvious that the member control was not up to the expected stantard. Customers were complaining about it and about its lack of usability. The intent of the company is also to sell online gym products, however it was not clear how they would be able to achieve this given the current design. How can we control members (registration, management), member portal and online store?

GOAL

  • Implement a robust and user friendly member system that ensures accurate and efficient handling of member information.
  • Delivery a unique the member portal to be intuitive, responsive and easy to navigate.
  • Ensure all services are well integrated within the portal.
  • Launch an online store that provides a seamless shopping experience for members

Design Process

Discover, Design, Develop and Delivery

Discover

Lean UX Canvas

I started the discovery phase of my process with the Lean UX canvas. This tool helped me proritize user needs over feature development, leading to a more user-centric solutions.

User research

I conducted a mix of qualitative and quantitative questions in order to understand the perceptions, preferences and behaviors of users. It will help me make informed decisions to improve my product service.

I interviewed high-income professionals, athletes or sports enthusiasts, luxury seekers that went to gympro or their competitor in the last 6 months.

Here are some key quantitative results

  • Overall satisfaction: 66% like the overall experience but is concern about the app usability
  • likelihood to recommended:" 66%
  • class information: 40%
  • online store: 40% want to buy online from the product brand
  • Personalized recommendations: 28%
