Ultrahuman

Transforming Complex Health Data into Actionable Wellness Insights

COMING SOON
YEAR 2025
BRIEFING

The health market, specifically smart rings like ultrahuman, Oura ring, and Whoop, represents a revolution in how we monitor our well-being. However, there is a significant opportunity to adapt and evolve this technology to better meet users' needs and realities.

Timeline

3 Months

ROLE

UX/UI Designer

PROCESS

Desk research, competitive analysis, interview, heuristic analysis, JTBD, HMW, Cost vs benefit matrix, MoSCoW method, hypothesis statement, journey map, moodboard, wireframing, user testing.

TOOLS

Figma, Maze, Meet

PROBLEM

Data Interpretation: We believe users struggle to understand the practical meaning of the data collected by the ring because the metrics are presented in a technical and decontextualized manner, resulting in low continuous engagement with the app.

GOAL
  • Design the dashboard with a focus on the practical meaning of the data.
  • Implement a layered visualization system (from simple to detailed).
  • Create personalized narratives that connect data to real-life contexts.

Research

Competitive Analysis

The key findings suggest Ultrahuman can differentiate through metabolic health focus, positioning between premium and budget competitors, and creating unique value through continuous glucose insights.

User Interviews

Conducted qualitative interview with 5 individuals who wears a smart ring, who are health conscius professional and fitness enthusiasts.

Life Change

"Stress! The ring changed my life. I changed my work schedule because I know there is a Client that stresses me. So I always move him to the day before my day off." P1

Awful UI

"The ring is promising tech, but the app experience is abysmal. There is way too much useless and confusing info. Stop making stuff up. Use common english terms"

Acessibility

"Unable to adjust font size in the app and the default size is too small to read for some people, incredibly disappointing. Cosidering return my ring"


Defining the solution

How might we translate complex health metrics into meaningful insights that support user retention and position the ring as a daily wellness companion?

Product hypothesis

By using product hypothesis, I was able to define my goals and antecipated results with grater clarity. They helped me focus on specific tasks and deepen my understanding of users need.

"We believe that implementing a layered dashboard that presents information from simple to detailed will help users better understand their health metrics without feeling overwhelmed because we know users currently feel lost in overly technical graphs and want to build confidence gradually."

"We believe that delivering personalized summaries and recommendations based on user patterns will increase user engagement and perceived value because users want actionable insights, not just raw numbers, to help connect data to real-life behaviors."

"We believe that offering a feature that helps users build daily habits based on ring data will increase long-term engagement and user satisfaction because users want to use the ring not just to track data, but to change behavior meaningfully."

Cost-Benefit Matrix

Prioritizing features based on user needs and business goals.

Customer Journey Map

The goal of this journey is to map the input and monitoring of data within the Ultrahuman app, identifying user actions, difficulties, and pain points, in order to then correlate these issues with the solutions proposed in the previous stages.

Business Goals

Metabolic

Deep metabolic insights through continuous glucose insights.

Sleep

Sleep optimization and recovery tracking.

Subscription

All without ongoing subscription costs.

Design

To push the boundaries of my design exploration, I incorporated an AI tool during the high-fidelity prototyping phase. This enabled me to rapidly generate visual concepts, experiment with layout variations, and refine aesthetic directions aligned with the project’s goals. The final designs were then crafted and polished in Figma, ensuring precision and consistency across the user interface.

Takeaways

Potential Impact

  • For Users:
  • Faster Clarity: One glance says better, worse, or steady.
  • Life style tracking: Arrows and % change reveal if new habits help or hurt.
  • For Business:
  • Increased Engagement: More daily open, deeper insights on metrics users care about.
  • Customer Retention: Personalized insights foster a deeper connection with users.

Next steps
