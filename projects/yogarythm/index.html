Yoga poses, or “asanas” are the physical postures practiced in yoga that help to promote strength, flexibility, and balance in the body. From the grounding Mountain Pose to the energizing Warrior Poses, each asana offers it’s unique baenefits for both the body and mind. Through mindful practice and breath awareness, yoga poses not only enhance physical well-being but also cultivate a sense of presence and inner peace, making them essential tools on the path to holistic health and wellness.
