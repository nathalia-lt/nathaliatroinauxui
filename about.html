About me

My professional journey began in healthcare, where I worked as a mental health nurse. During this time, I collaborated daily with psychiatrists and psychologists, discussing and analyzing patient behavior. This experience gave me a deep understanding of human motivations, needs, and emotions, as well as strong skills in active listening, empathy, and sensitivity to diverse profiles and contexts. In addition, I have lived in Brazil, London, and currently New York City, experiences that have broadened my worldview and strengthened my ability to adapt to different cultures. I carry a piece of each place with me, and this motivates me to seek solutions that respect and celebrate user diversity.
